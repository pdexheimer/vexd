Who did all this?

A joint effort between the Roskin and Weirauch labs at Cincinnati Children's Hospital, VExD was conceived and developed by:

  • Phillip Dexheimer
  • Mario Pujato
  • Krishna Roskin
  • Matthew Weirauch

Special thanks to Erica DePasquale for designing the VExD logo, Kevin Ernst for help with deploying the website, and all the members of the Roskin and Weirauch labs for their constructive feedback.
